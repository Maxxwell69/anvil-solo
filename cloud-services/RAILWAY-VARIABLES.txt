========================================
HOW TO ADD IN RAILWAY:
========================================

1. Go to https://railway.app/dashboard
2. Click on your project
3. Click on your SERVICE (not the database)
4. Click "Variables" tab
5. Click "+ New Variable" for each one
6. Copy the Variable Name
7. Copy the Value
8. Click "Add"
9. Repeat for all variables
10. Railway will auto-restart your service
